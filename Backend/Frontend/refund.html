Cancellation Policy

We understand that plans change. Here's our flexible cancellation policy:

Free Cancellation Window

Cancel your order free of charge up to 2 hours before the scheduled pickup time. Full refund will be issued to your original payment method.

More than 2 hours before pickup

100% refund - No cancellation fee

1-2 hours before pickup

50% cancellation fee applied

Less than 1 hour or after pickup

No refund available (service charges apply)

How to Cancel Your Order

  1. Through Your Account: Log in to your account, go to "My Orders," and click the "Cancel Order" button.
  2. Via Customer Support: Call us at +91 98765 43210 or email support@quicklaundry.com
  3. Mobile App: Open the app, navigate to your active orders, and select "Cancel Order."

Refund Policy

We process refunds fairly and promptly. Here are the scenarios where refunds apply:

Order Cancellation

If you cancel within the free cancellation window, you'll receive a full refund within 5-7 business days.

Service Not Delivered

If we fail to pick up or deliver your laundry as scheduled, you'll receive a 100% refund plus a 10% credit for future orders.

Poor Service Quality

If you're not satisfied with the cleaning quality, we'll re-clean at no charge or issue a partial/full refund based on the issue.

Service Unavailable

If we're unable to provide service due to operational issues, you'll receive an immediate full refund.

Refund Amount Calculation

Refunds are calculated based on the service stage at cancellation time and the reason for refund. Any applicable taxes will also be refunded.

Service Quality Issues

Your satisfaction is our priority. If you're not happy with our service, here's what we do:

Stains not removed: Free re-cleaning or 50% refund
Improper ironing: Free re-pressing or 30% refund
Unpleasant odor: Free re-wash or 40% refund
Color bleeding: 100% refund + compensation for damage
Delayed delivery: 10-20% discount on current order

Claim Reporting Timeline

Service quality issues must be reported within 24 hours of delivery. Please contact our customer support with photos or videos of the issue for faster resolution.

Damaged or Lost Items

While we take utmost care of your garments, in rare cases of damage or loss:

Issue Type Compensation Claim Process
Minor Damage Up to ₹500 compensation Submit photo + purchase receipt
Major Damage Up to ₹2,000 compensation Assessment by our team required
Lost Item Up to ₹3,000 compensation Investigation + proof of ownership
Valuables/Accessories Not covered (unless insured) Pre-declare high-value items

Important: Item Declaration

Items valued above ₹2,000 must be declared at the time of pickup. We offer optional insurance coverage for high-value garments (additional fee applies).

Filing a Damage/Loss Claim

  1. Report the issue within 24 hours of delivery
  2. Provide clear photos of the damaged item
  3. Submit proof of purchase (if available)
  4. Our team will assess and respond within 48 hours
  5. Compensation processed within 7-10 business days

Exceptions & Special Cases

Please note these important exceptions to our refund policy:

No Refund for Completed Services

Once laundry has been cleaned and delivered in good condition, refunds cannot be issued except for valid quality issues reported within 24 hours.

Pre-existing Damage

Items with pre-existing damage, stains, or wear-and-tear are not eligible for damage compensation. Please inform us about such items during pickup.

Promotional Discounts

Services availed under promotional offers or discount codes may have different refund terms. Check the specific terms of the promotion.

Third-Party Payments

For payments made through third-party platforms, refund processing may take longer and is subject to their policies.

Force Majeure

In cases of natural disasters, pandemics, government restrictions, or other unforeseen circumstances beyond our control, we reserve the right to modify our refund policy. Customers will be notified of any such changes.
