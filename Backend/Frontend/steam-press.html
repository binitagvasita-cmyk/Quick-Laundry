Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our steam press service

What is steam pressing?

Steam pressing uses high-temperature steam to remove wrinkles and freshen clothes without direct heat contact, making it ideal for delicate fabrics like silk, wool, and synthetic blends.

How long does the service take?

Standard service is completed within 24 hours. We also offer express 12-hour service for urgent requirements at a small additional charge.

Is steam pressing safe for all fabrics?

Yes! Steam pressing is gentle and safe for most fabrics including silk, wool, cotton, linen, and synthetic materials. Our experts identify each fabric type before pressing.

Do you provide pickup and delivery?

Absolutely! We offer free pickup and delivery at your doorstep. Just schedule a convenient time slot and our team will be there.

How is pricing calculated?

Pricing is per piece and varies by garment type. Select your service from the dropdown and you'll see the exact price before confirming your order.
