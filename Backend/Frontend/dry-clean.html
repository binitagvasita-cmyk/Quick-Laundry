Why Dry Clean?

When Should You Choose Dry Cleaning?

Dry cleaning is essential for garments that cannot tolerate water — such as suits, silk sarees, heavy embroidered outfits, and wool coats. Our professional process removes stains and odours without harming the fabric.

Silk, wool, velvet and delicate fabrics
Heavy embroidered or embellished garments
Garments with "Dry Clean Only" label
Stubborn stains that won't wash out
Items that wrinkle or shrink in water
