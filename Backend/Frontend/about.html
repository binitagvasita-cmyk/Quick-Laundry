Our Story

How It All Started

Founded in 2018, Quick Laundry began with a simple mission: to revolutionize the way people experience laundry services. What started as a small operation with just two dedicated team members has grown into a thriving business serving over 10,000 happy customers.

We noticed that busy professionals and families struggled to find time for laundry. We created a solution that brings professional laundry care right to your doorstep, saving you time and ensuring your clothes receive the care they deserve.
