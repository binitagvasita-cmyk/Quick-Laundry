Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions

What is the minimum order quantity?

We accept orders for a minimum of 1 piece. There's no maximum limit - we can handle bulk orders too!

Do I need to wash clothes before ironing?

Yes, this is an iron-only service. Please ensure your clothes are already washed and completely dry before pickup.

What if I'm not satisfied with the service?

We offer 100% satisfaction guarantee. If you're not happy, we'll re-iron your clothes free of charge within 24 hours.

Is urgent service available on weekends?

Yes! Our urgent service is available 7 days a week, including weekends and most holidays.

How do you handle delicate fabrics?

We use temperature-controlled irons and special techniques for delicate fabrics like silk, satin, and linen to prevent damage.

Why can I only book 5 days in advance?

To ensure timely service and smooth scheduling for all customers, we accept bookings up to 5 days in advance. This helps our team manage pickups efficiently and guarantee quality service.
